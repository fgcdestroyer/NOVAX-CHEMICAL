Who We Are

Built on Chemistry.
Driven by Trust.

NOVAX CHEMICAL FZE LLC is a fully licensed chemical trading company established within the prestigious Ajman Nuventures Centre Free Zone, United Arab Emirates. We specialize in the wholesale import and export of a broad spectrum of chemical products.

Our operations span industrial chemicals, pharmaceutical-grade compounds, oil well chemicals, synthetic materials, fertilizers, acids, alkalines, detergents, disinfectants, and premium fragrance chemicals.

Headquartered at the iconic Amber Gem Tower in Ajman, we combine a strategic free zone location with deep industry expertise to deliver unmatched chemical sourcing and distribution solutions worldwide.
